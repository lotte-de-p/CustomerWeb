Welcome to Telenet

We will activate your SIM card. Your old SIM card will continue to work for the time being.

Did you choose to transfer your number from another operator?

Within 24 hours, you will receive a text message from Telenet to say that your number is active.

Then follow these steps:

  1. Turn off your phone
  2. Exchange your old SIM card for your new one
  3. Enter the PIN code and you're good to go!

Do you have a new number or do you remain a Telenet customer with a new SIM card?

Insert your new SIM card into your phone. You can get started within a few minutes!
